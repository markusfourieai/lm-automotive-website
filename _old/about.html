Our Story

The L&M Story

L&M Automotive Performance was founded with one clear vision: to deliver exceptional automotive care that goes beyond the ordinary. What started as a small, passionate workshop in Stellenbosch has grown into one of the Western Cape's most respected performance automotive centres.

Our journey began with a deep love for cars and an even deeper commitment to the people who drive them. Over the years, we have earned the trust of hundreds of clients — from daily commuters who need reliable servicing to motorsport enthusiasts who demand race-ready precision.

Based in Plankenburg, Stellenbosch, we are conveniently located to serve the greater Winelands and Cape Town area. We invest continuously in training and equipment so that we can offer our clients the very best in modern automotive care.
